How to Use This App

This is a simple flashcard app. You can:

  • Enter a term and definition to create flashcards
  • Click "Flip" to see the definition
  • Click "Next" to move to the next card
  • Use the search bar to find matching cards

This app uses a list (array) to store flashcards and lets you interact with them.
